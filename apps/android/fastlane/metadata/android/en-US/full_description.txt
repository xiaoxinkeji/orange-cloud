Orange Cloud is a native Cloudflare client for Android phones and tablets. Sign in with Cloudflare's official OAuth, choose the permissions you want to grant, and manage your domains from anywhere. No API tokens to create, copy, or paste.

SIGN IN, THE SAFE WAY
- Official OAuth 2.0 with PKCE: authorize in the browser, just like on the web
- You pick the scopes; the app only gets what you grant
- Tokens are stored in the Android Keystore and never leave your device
- API requests go straight from your device to Cloudflare. No middleman servers, no data collection

DOMAINS & DNS
- Every domain at a glance, with status and plan
- Full DNS record management: add, edit, delete, search
- One-tap proxy toggle (the orange cloud itself)

ANALYTICS
- Requests, bandwidth, cache hit rate, and blocked threats
- Clean charts: the last 24 hours free, 7-day and 30-day ranges with Pro

WORKERS
- Browse deployed scripts, details, and metrics
- Pro: stream live logs over WebSocket with an ongoing notification

STORAGE (PRO)
- R2: browse buckets and objects, upload and download
- D1: run queries in a built-in SQL console, browse and edit table rows
- KV: read, write, and delete keys

SECURITY & NETWORK (PRO)
- WAF custom rules: review, create, and toggle them on the go
- Cloudflare Tunnel: check status and active connections

MADE FOR ANDROID
- Day-and-night theme that shifts with the time of day, with Material You dynamic color
- Adaptive two-pane layout for tablets and foldables
- Home-screen shortcuts (long-press the icon) and a Quick Settings tile
- Predictive back and per-app language

FREE VS PRO
Free, forever: one account, full domain and DNS management, and 24-hour analytics.
Orange Cloud Pro unlocks multiple accounts, the entire Storage tab (R2 / D1 / KV), live Workers logs, WAF rules, Tunnel, and 7-day / 30-day analytics. Available as a monthly or yearly subscription (yearly includes a 7-day free trial) or a one-time lifetime purchase.

Subscriptions are billed through Google Play and renew automatically unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Manage or cancel anytime in Google Play.
